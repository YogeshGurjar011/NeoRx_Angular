Why Neo Rx



Children with ASD require special attention and long-term dedicated care, which will affect other commitments of some parents. Neo Rx can be a time- saving tool by involving the child in everyday therapy sessions without the presence of a parent.


Early intervention of this therapy has shown to be effective in improving the development of children with autism and the prevention of secondary conditions.

Digitalizing the treatment (ASD) with the help of our app,
Neo Rx, can provide you with the following benefits:

Communication

Bridge the communication gap between parent and child.

E-learning

E-learning approaches can significantly widen the availability of ASD treatment.

Proper programs

Use of proper ABA treatment programs designed by skilled professionals (BCBA)

Art activities

Drawing, coloring, stickers,
Games, and Puzzles

Easy entry process

Reducing caregiver pain-points (material and data collection not needed)

Affordable Therapy

Address the prohibitive cost of autism treatment and the severe shortage of trained therapists.
