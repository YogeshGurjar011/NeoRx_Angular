Right learning at the Right Time
is very important.

Our digital therapy app involves a machine learning (ML) mechanism by which children can constantly learn and improve without intervention by human therapists. It provides personalized programs to fit the needs of each child, and AI gives instructions according to the child’s progress.

The Neo Rx app uses face tracking, eye tracking and body movements to obtain behavior-related information from each child, and it uses it to reinforce learning through visual tracking, motor imitation, actions and visual skills..

The Expert Speaks

Neo-Rx has been created to provide supplementary special education for children with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental delays. Children require high quality services for an extended time to have a positive effect on their life.

Our goal is to provide children with skills that will lead them to being placed in the least restrictive setting possible and to increase their independence. The application was created based on the science of applied behavior analysis, and it uses evidence-based procedures to maximize learning for the child.
