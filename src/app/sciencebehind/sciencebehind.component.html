Science behind the App

The focus is on four main skill domains: social, behavioral, verbal and academic.

There are several levels and sub-levels within each skill domain. Each user will be asked questions, beginning with the preliminary level of each level. Based on the data analysis of the user’s answers to the assessment questions, the application will decide the level in which the user begins instruction. The application keeps precise records of the data based on the child’s responses throughout the assessment and instruction.

Features of Neo Rx

The application offers visual schedules, a tracker and a token economy to track behaviors and instruction progress. It uses eye-tracking and movement-tracking to teach visual tracking and motor imitation and to demonstrate actions and social skills. The ABA principles used in the application include generality, effective technology, applied conceptually, and a systematic, analytic and behavioral approach..

Understanding Autism

What is Autism?

Autism, also known as autism spectrum disorder (ASD), refers to a wide range of symptoms, including challenges related to social skills, repetitive behavior, speech and nonverbal communication. The ways in which people with autism learn, think and solve problems ranges from highly effective to entirely ineffective.

Indications of autism usually appear by age 2 or 3 for those on the autism spectrum disorder. Some related development delays can appear earlier and are diagnosed as early as 18 months. Research shows that early intervention can greatly improve the chances for positive outcomes later in life for people with autism..

GOLD-STANDARD TREATMENT FOR AUTISM

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a type of therapeutic intervention for people with psychological conditions, which helps improve social, communication and learning skills through reinforcement strategies. ABA therapy is an evidence-based method that is based on the fact that we all learn through the consequences of behavior..

In other words, if you want your child to learn how to behave in a certain way (such as sitting still) and you reward them when they do so (such as with a treat), then those behaviors will be more likely to become habits over time
