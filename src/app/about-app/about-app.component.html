Our solution: Neo Rx

Neo Rx is a digital application, intended to be a non-drug prescription approach for autism spectrum disorder (ASD), based on ABA therapy.

Digital Therapeutics (DTx) and Autism Therapy

Our digital therapy app involves a machine learning (ML) mechanism by which children can constantly learn and improve without intervention by human therapists. It provides personalized programs to fit the needs of each child, and AI instruction progresses according to the child’s progress. We use deep data mining to collect progress data for each child and display it in a way that is easily understood by parents.

The Neo Rx app uses face tracking, eye tracking and body movements to obtain behavior-related information from each child, and it uses it to reinforce learning through visual tracking, motor imitation, actions and visual skills. Furthermore, our app will address the high cost of autism treatment and the severe shortage of trained therapists. Our objective is to deliver highly effective therapy at a fraction of the cost of traditional therapy, anytime, anywhere.

We believe in empowering every child by understanding individual needs and by creating a personalized program to help them maximize their independence in daily life, in their own unique way.

How does Neo Rx work?

Autism Diagnosis

The Autism Diagnostic Observation Schedule (ADOS-2) is a standardized assessment of communication, social interaction, play and behaviors in children. You can consult your clinician for a detailed diagnosis.

Onboarding and Usage

1

REGISTRATION AND LOGIN

The child or the parent/guardian will register basic information. A username and password will be created for each user to manage their account and to provide personalized learning instructions.

2

PARENT FEEDBACK FORM

The parent will first fill out a questionnaire and a parent feedback form, on which they will answer all questions related to their child. This will help in creating a personalized assessment for the child and it will assist in maximizing the learning experience.

3

ASSESSMENT

The child will complete an assessment based on VB-MAPP, in the presence of a parent/guardian. There will be questions from each skill domain, such as conditional reinforcement for stimuli/matching, listener responding, echoic and tact, which will determine the individualized placement of the child based on their current skill set.

4

LEARNING

Based on the child’s assessment score, we will create a customized learning session every day of 60-90 minutes, designed according to ABA therapy principles. It will cover social, verbal, behavioral and academic domains.,
There will be an avatar chosen by the child to guide him or her through the entire journey, with voice-overs and videos. The app uses positive reinforcement based on responses through fun games, videos, social attention, praise, edibles and tokens. There are short quizzes after every bucket.

5

PROGRESS REPORT

The app uses multiple data points, such as a learning score, responsiveness, etc. to provide an AI generated personalized report. The report comprises the learning report and the quiz score from each skill domain, along with the time taken to complete the report.

Follow Up

There will be a post-assessment presented to the user by a clinician/physician after 3 to 6 months to determine the efficacy of the application for the user.

Compatible Devices

This app is compatible with the minimum requirement of iOS version 12.0 and Android version SDK 19 KitKat.
