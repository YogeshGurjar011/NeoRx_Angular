Potential Technical problems:

I forgot my password?
Click on “Forgot my password” at the bottom of the NEO Rx login page and enter your e-mail address. A link will be sent to your email ID. You can click on the link and reset your password and log in.

How can I give feedback?
You can give feedback and report your comments, suggestions and complaints about the app by using the Feedback section on www.neo-rx.com or by sending an e-mail to contactus@neo-rx.com.

The application was deleted?
If your app is deleted, you can download Neo Rx on the AppStore for your iOS devices and on Google PlayStore for your Android devices. Once you have logged into your account with your membership information, you can easily access past reports and statistics about your child’s performance. You can resume the therapy session from where you left off..

The application is stuck or frozen?
Please try to close and then reopen the application. If this doesn’t work, please uninstall and reinstall the app, make sure there is an active internet connection to do so. If that doesn’t resolve the issue, please contact us by email and provide us with the specifications of the device and the OS version so that we can help you with the issue.
If the problem is still not resolved after these steps, check to see if your application is up to date. If it is not, please install the update.
If you have tried all of the above but there is still no improvement, please contact us.

How do I know if the app is able to hear me during a verbal interaction question?

There is a green microphone that will be visible on the screen. In case the microphone is not entirely visible for consecutive verbal questions, kindly close the app and reopen it. Also, ensure that no other app that uses a microphone is open simultaneously..
