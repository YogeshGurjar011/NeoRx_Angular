We are constantly working to make the world better for children with autism

Every child undergoes a unique process of cognitive development. Some are quick learners, while some take more time to progress. However, children who are diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) often face a multitude of difficulties, which may negatively affect their learning and their behavioral skills.


Impute has developed a digital therapy solution to address your child’s learning needs and to help in their overall development.


We believe that our digital therapy app, Neo Rx, will be very effective in helping children and parents cope with autism and learning disabilities. Our therapeutic system is designed to assess children’s needs, and to then provide them with the best possible personalized therapy..

Our Mission

“Transform the quality of life of children who have ASD to improve their physical and mental health with various digital behavioral approaches that are clinically designed by our team of autism experts. The learning-through-play method (digitalizing ABA therapy) will make parenting easier.”
